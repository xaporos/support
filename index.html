Support Guidelines

Having trouble with our app? Follow these steps to resolve your issue:

  1. Update the app to the latest version.
  2. Check the About section within the app for guidelines on how to use the app
  3. If your issue persists, contact us via email.
  4. When contacting us, please include:
    • Your device type and model
    • Operating system version
    • A detailed description of the issue

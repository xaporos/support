Support Guidelines

Follow these steps to get support for our mobile app:

  1. Ensure the app is updated to the latest version.
  2. Check the About section within the app for guidelines on how to use the app
  3. If your issue persists, contact us via email.
  4. Provide details such as:
    • Device type and model
    • Operating system version
    • A brief description of the issue
